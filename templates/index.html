About Us



The app was created for people who have symptoms of lung cancer, support team members of cancer diaganosis centers and assist docters and radiological experts to use High resolution Computed Tomography (HRCT) Images for lung cancer detection and further decision making from visual inspection. It helps you provides tools for cancer detection and forums for asking questions, connecting docters, and managing medications. The app is a great way to find early stage of cancer and to communicate with healthcare providers and to request support from friends and family.
